Multi-Family
Wolftree Properties actively acquires class B multi-family properties throughout the Eastern US, with an emphasis on Connecticut, Massachusetts, and New York. We aggressively pursue opportunities with deferred maintenance and above-market vacancy as our in-depth knowledge of the construction process allows us to add value to the physical assets and the communities as a whole.
Self-Storage
Wolftree Properties seeks to acquire value-add self-storage assets on behalf of our LP investors. We specialize in identifying mom & pop ownership entities in markets with low supply and high entitlement costs.
Development
In today's development environment—characterized by increasing construction costs and rising interest rates—we focus on identifying opportunities in markets with long-term growth in mind. Product types include multi-family and self-storage, along with select retail and industrial opportunities where we have a tenant-in-hand. We are active in Fairfield County, CT, through the Bronx, NY, and have been pursuing sites inside the 128 within the suburban Boston marketplace.
Expertise
The principals of Wolftree Properties have executed $100MM of transactions over the past four years. Our competitive advantages lie in identifying off-market opportunities and finding value in properties which others miss. We achieve this through comprehensive market due-diligence, meticulous financial modeling, and creative deal-making.
